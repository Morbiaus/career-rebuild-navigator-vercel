Operating model

Built like a small elite job placement agency.

The workflow mirrors real recruiting work: intake, positioning, sourcing, scoring, briefing, resume tailoring, outreach, tracking, and quality review.

  • Finds focused role lanes instead of dumping hundreds of jobs.
  • Explains why each role fits and what gaps to watch.
  • Prepares a practical 30-day application plan.
AI placement team

The agents do the work a placement agency would normally do.

The MVP simulates the agency workflow in-browser. Future versions can connect live job APIs, resume upload, OpenAI/OpenClaw generation, and saved application tracking.
