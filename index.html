How it works

A practical workflow for the first 30 days after displacement.

The first version is intentionally simple: enter your career triggers, receive a fit-based plan, and use the output to guide applications, recruiter outreach, and resume tailoring.

1

Enter triggers

Salary, work model, level, target lane, skills, runway, and background.

2

Score fit

The matching engine compares the profile against role families and sample job targets.

3

Prepare materials

The tool creates positioning, resume angle, and a recruiter note.

4

Act for 30 days

Use the plan to apply with focus, track responses, and adjust based on traction.

AI placement team

The agents mirror the work of a small job placement agency.

This is the operating model we can later connect to live job APIs, resume upload, OpenAI/OpenClaw generation, and saved application tracking.

01

Candidate Intake Agent

Captures salary, remote preference, skills, level, runway, location, and constraints.

02

Positioning Agent

Translates experience into target lanes, transferable skills, and market language.

03

Job Search Agent

Uses a sample market now; later connects approved job feeds.

04

Fit Scoring Agent

Scores jobs against salary, work model, skills, seniority, and urgency.

05

Job Briefing Agent

Explains fit, watch items, application angle, and next action.

06

Resume Tailoring Agent

Suggests role-specific summary language and bullet themes.

07

Outreach Agent

Drafts a recruiter message the candidate can copy, edit, and send.

08

QA / Governance Agent

Flags assumptions, protects against overclaiming, and keeps human review in the loop.

Career rebuild assessment

Enter your triggers. The AI placement team returns a focused plan.

Use realistic inputs. The stronger the triggers, the better the match logic. This MVP uses built-in sample roles until live job feeds are connected.

Avoid sensitive personal data. This MVP does not need SSNs, addresses, birthdates, or private documents.
Important: This tool supports job-search planning. It is not an employer, staffing firm, legal advisor, or guarantee of employment. Users should verify all job postings and review all AI-generated material before using it.
